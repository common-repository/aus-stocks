Display your Australian Stock Exchange (ASX) Share Price, Graph and Announcements automatically on your website.

== Description ==


Aus Stocks allows you to display your company ASX, NSX or NZX share price, interactive price history graph and announcements on your WordPress website.
Free Version
* Display of simple share price

Standard Version
*Display of Share Price daily trading information and share price graph

Pro Version
* All of the above plus the display of all Announcements as they are lodged.
* Integration with Mailchimp mailing list


-   Announcements are added automatically shortly after you lodge them with the stock exchange.
-   Simple shortcodes allow you to display them anywhere on your site.
-   Stock data is supplied on a subscription basis, please visit <a href="https://ausstocks.com.au" target="_blank">https://ausstocks.com.au</a> to sign up and for further information.		


== Installation ==

how to install

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/ausstocks` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the Settings->Aus Stocks screen to configure the plugin
4. Enter your stock code and stock exchange.
5. Then use the shortcode [asx_basic] to display the share price on your page.

Data is supplied on a subscription basis, please visit <a href="https://ausstocks.com.au" target="_blank">https://ausstocks.com.au</a> to sign up and for further information.
